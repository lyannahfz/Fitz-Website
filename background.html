About Fitz

Hello! I'm Mohd Fitrah Abdul Rahman, or affectionately known as Fitz, and I'm 24 years old talented designer from Kuching, Sarawak. An alumnus of Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kampus Samarahan, I recently completed my Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Design that focus on Advertising and currently pursuing a Master's in Visual Communication and New Media in Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Puncak Alam.

As a freelance designer, I have been working with a well-known businesses that creating unforgettable mark with my inventive ideas. I also writes food reviews for Borneo Food Hunter, where I expresses my passion for local cuisine.

My journey encourages others to pursue their dreams with determination. I emphasizes the value of creativity, storytelling, and self-belief, and uses my abilities to build connections as well as promote changes in the community. I want to urge an aspiring designers to embrace their individuality and pursue excellence with confidence.
